Dealing with a diagnosis of any kind that threatens your life can leave you in a state of shock, worry, and fear. It can be difficult to not allow it to consume your every thought. And those thoughts can easily point to a doom and gloom scenario.

Leaning on friends and family and finding activities that make you happy can help to calm your fears.
