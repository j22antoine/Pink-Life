What led to this diagnosis was a series of tests which started with an annual mammogram. I had been getting mammograms since I was 35 because my mother was diagnosed with Breast Cancer that year. At 41, this annual test had become a mere formality. I never thought I'd get cancer!

Celebrate Your Wins

Survivorship is a very important part of this journey, that none of us have chosen. Celebrate every win you experience through this journey. Whether it's a promising test result or you simply having a good day celebrate it! People do this by documenting their journey. celebrating their yearly 'Cancerversary'
