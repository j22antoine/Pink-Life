Keeping the news of a diagnosis to your self might be your natural response.

Find a Treatment Team

After diagnosis, it will be imperative that you schedule consultations. If you're lucky, there will be a cancer center or hospital known with a good reputation for treating your type of Breast Cancer. If not, do your research and set up a consultation with a breast surgeon asap. Once you land on where you will get treatment, a team of doctors will be assembled for your care. Different doctors and support staff that you may encounter are a Breast Surgeon, Oncologist, Radiologist, Plastic Surgeon, Nurse Navigator, Social Worker, etc.

Celebrate Your Wins

Survivorship is a very important part of this journey, that none of us have chosen. Celebrate every win you experience through this journey. Whether it's a promising test result or you simply having a good day celebrate it! People do this by documenting their journey. celebrating their yearly 'Cancerversary'
